Typography

Bebas Neue

Bebas Neue will be used as the font for all headers, and also the name and navigation bar. We chose this font as it is bold, pops out, is still professional. It draws the visitors attention right to any headline.

Roboto

Roboto will be used for the paragraph text throughout the website. We chose this font as it is easy to read with its modern style and nice letter spacing. This will help our visitors to enjoy their time while reading about upcoming events and weather updates.
