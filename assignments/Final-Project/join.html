Join the Chamber of Commerce

Invest in your business. Invest in your economy. Grow as a community.

  Here at the Salt Lake City Chamber of Commerce, we're the foremost organization in Utah to help your business learn and grow. It doesn't matter whether you are a "mom and pop shop" or a large, national corporation, will help your business take that next step.

  We will work with you to understand your objectives, and help you find the right package to maximize your exposure and achieve your goals.

  If you would like to learn more, please review our membership options and fill out the form below.

Non-Profit

  • Benefits:

  • - Unlimited registrations to Chamber Lunch and Business After Hours event
  • - Prime-positioned website listing in Chamber Business Directory
  • - Access to Sponsorship Level Packages
  • - Eligibility for Chamber Board of Directors
