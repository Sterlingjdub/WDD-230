Adventure Tours

Adventure is out there!

"Life is either a daring adventure or nothing."
~Helen Keller

Too many times, we get caught up in the day to day duties of life, and don't take time to enjoy ourselves and the world around us.
Now's your chance to do both. Come and join us on one of our many weekend Adventure Tours!

Emerald Lake

  • Days: Friday-Sunday
  • Length: 10 total miles
  • Skill Level Required: Intermediate
  • Cost: $ 25.00
  • Located in Bozeman, some consider this the best ride in Montana. There is a stiff, 2k ft climb to a gorgeous high alpine lake. This of course makes for a killer downhill on the return.

Grassy Mountain Loop

  • Days: Saturday-Sunday
  • Length: 16 total miles
  • Skill Level Required: Intermediate
  • Cost: $ 20.00
  • Located in Bozeman, this loop is a long, beautiful ride with many meadows and switchbacks along the way.

Lick Creek

  • Days: Wednesday, Thursday, Saturday
  • Length: 9 total miles
  • Skill Level Required: Beginner
  • Cost: $ 15.00
  • Located in Bozeman, this loop will take you on a relaxing, scenic ride right along Lick Creek. Along the way are a couple of resting areas perfect for picnics.
