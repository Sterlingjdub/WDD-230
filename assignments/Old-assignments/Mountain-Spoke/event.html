Cycle the Continental Divide

July 9 - 24, 2018

  • Dates: July 9-24, 2018
  • No entry fee
  • Basic Equipment:Glasses, helmet, GPS, lights, hydration systems, tire pump and patches.
  • Route:The Continental Divide bike route is a long-distance, off-road bicycle touring route from Banff, Alberta, Canada to Antelope Wells, New Mexico, USA. As of 2016, the route is 2768.4 miles (4455.3 km) long.
    The Continental Divide is routed through a variety of terrain and geographic features. Highlights include the Flathead Valley in British Columbia; Grand Teton National Park and the Great Divide Basin in Wyoming; South Park and Boreas Pass in Colorado; and Polvadera Mesa and the Gila Wilderness in New Mexico
